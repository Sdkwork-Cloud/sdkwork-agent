# Photorealistic Template

For hyper-realistic photography-quality images.

## Template Structure

**Tier 1:** Detailed subject with physical specifics
**Tier 2:** Real environment with natural elements  
**Tier 3:** Natural lighting with physical accuracy
**Tier 4:** Specific camera gear and settings
**Tier 5:** Photography style references
**Tier 6:** Maximum quality descriptors

## Key Elements

- Real camera models and lenses
- Accurate aperture and settings
- Natural lighting only
- Physics-accurate descriptions
- Photography genre references
- Photo-specific quality terms

## Example

Professional portrait of elderly fisherman, weathered face with deep wrinkles and silver beard, kind blue eyes with crow's feet, wearing yellow rain slicker, holding worn wooden oars, standing on weathered dock at dawn, calm harbor water with fishing boats, soft golden sunrise light from horizon, warm side lighting, shallow depth of field, shot on Canon EOS R5 with 85mm f/1.2 lens, f/1.8 aperture, sharp focus on eyes, creamy bokeh background, documentary photography style, National Geographic quality, 8K resolution, photorealistic, hyper-detailed skin texture
