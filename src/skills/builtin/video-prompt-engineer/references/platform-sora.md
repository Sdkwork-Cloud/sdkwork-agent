# Platform Guide - Sora
Sora video generation platform guide.

---

## Platform Overview

**Company:** OpenAI
**Strengths:**
- Long video generation (up to 60 seconds+)
- Excellent temporal coherence
- Complex scene understanding
- Good physics simulation

**Best For:**
- Long-form videos
- Complex stories with multiple scenes
- Physics-heavy content
- Professional storytelling

---

## Key Parameters

### Duration
- **Minimum:** 2 seconds
- **Maximum:** 60+ seconds (depending on access)
- **Recommended:** 10-30 seconds

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** (landscape, default)
- **9:16** (vertical, portrait)
- **1:1** (square)
- **21:9** (cinematic)

---

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Think in scenes:** Sora handles multi-scene videos well
2. **Describe the story:** Good for narrative content
3. **Be specific about physics:** Sora understands physics well
4. **Use longer durations:** Sora excels at longer videos

---

## Negative Prompts for Sora

```
text, watermark, subtitles, blurry, low quality, distorted, bad anatomy, inconsistent characters, impossible physics, illogical
```
