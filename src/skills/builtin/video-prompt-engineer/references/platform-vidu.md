# Platform Guide - Vidu (维度)
Vidu (维度) video generation platform guide.

---

## Platform Overview

**Company:** Vidu (维度)
**Strengths:**
- High quality cinematic output
- Excellent visual details
- Good character consistency
- Professional cinematography

**Best For:**
- High-quality cinematic videos
- Professional productions
- Detailed visual scenes

---

## Key Parameters

### Duration
- **Minimum:** 2 seconds
- **Maximum:** 60 seconds (depending on plan)
- **Recommended:** 5-15 seconds

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** (landscape, default)
- **9:16** (vertical, portrait)
- **1:1** (square)
- **21:9** (cinematic)

### Resolutions
- **720p** (fast)
- **1080p** (recommended)
- **4K** (high quality)

---

## Tips & Best Practices

1. **Use cinematic language:** Vidu responds well to cinematic descriptions
2. **Include camera movement:** Makes videos more dynamic
3. **Describe lighting in detail:** Important for mood
4. **Keep it 5-15 seconds:** Optimal for quality

---

## Negative Prompts for Vidu

```
text, watermark, subtitles, blurry, low quality, distorted, bad anatomy, inconsistent characters, jerky motion
```
