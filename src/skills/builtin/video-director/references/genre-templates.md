# Genre Templates

Genre templates for different film types.

---

## Drama (剧情)

**Mood:** Emotional, realistic, character-driven

**Common Elements:**
- Conflicts and relationships
- Character growth and development
- Emotional journeys
- Realistic settings

**Camera Style:**
- Medium shots for dialogue
- Close-ups for emotion
- Natural lighting

---

## Romance (浪漫)

**Mood:** Warm, intimate, tender

**Common Elements:**
- Love stories
- Emotional connections
- Beautiful settings
- Soft lighting

**Camera Style:**
- Soft focus
- Warm color palette
- Slow camera movements
- Close-ups of faces and hands

---

## Comedy (喜剧)

**Mood:** Light, funny, energetic

**Common Elements:**
- Humor and wit
- Exaggerated situations
- Timing and pacing
- Bright colors

**Camera Style:**
- Quick cuts
- Wide shots for physical comedy
- Bright lighting

---

## Suspense/Thriller (悬疑/惊悚)

**Mood:** Tense, mysterious, unsettling

**Common Elements:**
- Mystery and suspense
- Tension building
- Unexpected twists
- Dark atmospheres

**Camera Style:**
- Low angles
- Dutch angles
- Slow movements
- Shadows and darkness

---

## Action (动作)

**Mood:** Exciting, fast-paced, dynamic

**Common Elements:**
- High energy
- Stunts and fights
- Quick pacing
- Dynamic camera work

**Camera Style:**
- Quick cuts
- Handheld camera
- Tracking shots
- High energy movements

---

## Sci-Fi (科幻)

**Mood:** Futuristic, imaginative, grand

**Common Elements:**
- Futuristic settings
- Advanced technology
- Imaginative worlds
- Epic scale

**Camera Style:**
- Wide shots for scale
- Cool color palette
- Modern lighting
- Epic camera movements

---

## Fantasy (奇幻)

**Mood:** Magical, imaginative, epic

**Common Elements:**
- Magical elements
- Mythical creatures
- Epic worlds
- Hero's journey

**Camera Style:**
- Epic wide shots
- Rich colors
- Dramatic lighting
- Grand camera movements

---

## Documentary (纪录片)

**Mood:** Realistic, informative, authentic

**Common Elements:**
- Real stories
- Interviews
- Real locations
- Authenticity

**Camera Style:**
- Handheld
- Natural lighting
- Interview shots
- B-roll footage

---

## Historical/Period (历史/年代)

**Mood:** Authentic, nostalgic, period-specific

**Common Elements:**
- Period costumes
- Historical settings
- Authentic details
- Period-appropriate language

**Camera Style:**
- Classic framing
- Warm or muted colors
- Traditional camera work
- Attention to detail
